Registration Fees

If your paper was accepted, at least one of the author's must register at the same time that the camera ready version or the paper is submitted, or the paper will be pulled from the proceedings. There are NO refunds on registration.

NOTE: Papers will be pulled from the proceedings if at least one author does not register and pay.
NOTE: If your name is on two papers - you can present only one and your co-author will need to present the other.
NOTE: All program committee members who attend need to register and pay. There is no fee waiver.
NOTE: All people receiving travel scholarships and graduate student consortium grants must still register and pay for the conference and any tutorials they are interested in attending. See the Scholarship Page for more information.

Tutorial Schedule

Scholarships

Conference participants can apply for Travel Scholarships during registration. Level of participation in the conference, need, and career level will be considered when reviewing applications. Scholarships are capped. They are based on actual expenses and are not a fixed amount. See the Scholarship Page for more information.
